Day 1:
My first Impression to Yelp

So I just sign up my first Yelp account a week ago. Obviously, for the job application.

Posted by Yizzy Wu on June 7, 2017

To be honest, as a person who move to New York two years ago, I barely use Yelp.com. I know When people want to find some places to eat, it’s always an intuition to open Yelp and check, however, for being lazy in most of the time, I pass this job to my friends, who care and trust more about the reviews and rankings of the restaurants. On the other hand, maybe another reason is that I don’t have a good "Yelp Luck”— I choose unwisely all the time. The thing is when I tried some of the most highest reviewed restaurants, the foods were just not that good as the reviewers recommended, I ended up feeling disappointed. …..Or on the second thought, maybe I just have a different taste from other reviewers that’s all.

So I just registered my first Yelp account a week ago. Obviously, for the job application. I even made my first review of a seafood restaurant I went few days ago.

The experience through the registration wasn’t the best experience in my life as I remembered.

So, since I got this chance to explore the whole platform of Yelp, I decided that I would like to do a detailed walkthrough as the Yelp’s newcomer to have a better picture of the process and what’s going on of the website.

So Let’s get started!
